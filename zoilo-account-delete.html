How to Delete Your Account

Steps to Delete Your Account

To delete your account and associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to the Settings menu by tapping on the gear icon in the top left corner.
  3. Scroll down and find the "Delete Account" button.
  4. Tap the "Delete Account" button and follow the prompts to confirm account deletion.

If you have any issues, please contact our support team at help@zoiloapp.com.
